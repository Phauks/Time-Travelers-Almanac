Back to the Future

1985 · Film · dir. Robert Zemeckis · trilogy

The Rule

MutableThe Palimpsest — one timeline, redrawn

The Mode

ContraptionDeLorean · 1.21 GW · 88 mph

Loop status

NoneLinear jumps, no repetition

What you're allowed to do

Everything — and that's the danger. This is a single, overwritable timeline. Change the past and the present redraws itself around you. Prevent your parents from meeting and you begin, quite literally, to fade from the family photograph.

Paradox exposure High

Field note: keep the almanac dry and the flux capacitor charged. If you meet yourself, do not shake hands — introductions can wait until you're certain which of you is load-bearing.

Mechanism

A plutonium-fuelled contraption reaches 88 mph and displaces the vehicle (and occupants) to a set date. Later instalments substitute a Mr. Fusion and a hover-conversion, but the rule never changes: the timeline is clay.
